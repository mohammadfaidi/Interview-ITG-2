- Database Normalization: Normalization is a database design technique which organizes tables in a manner that reduces redundancy and dependency of data. It divides larger tables to smaller tables and links them using relationships.

- First Normal Form (1NF): Remove Repeated Group (multi-value).
- Second Normal Form (2NF): 1NF + Remove Partial Dependency.
- Third Normal Form (3NF): 2NF + Remove Transitive Dependency.
